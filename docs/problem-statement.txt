Project Implementation Guidelines
The FYP & Degree Workflow Automation Portal is a complete academic management system designed to digitize all manual processes, reduce delays, and ensure transparent tracking for:
Final Year Project (FYP) Lifecycle
Idea - Supervisor - Proposal - SRS - Internal - External.
Degree Completion Workflow: Replaces 4 manual clearance forms with a unified automated system
